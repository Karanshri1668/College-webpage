About Us

We are a public Trust duly registered under the Provisions of Bombay Public Trust Act 1950. We are known and Recognized as GNYANODAYA EDUCATION TRUST. We the persons associated with the Trust in any capacity possess a sufficient Long experience in the field of Education and Social Service to its credit.

Mission

To inspire and empower students to become lifelong learners, engaged citizens, and ethical leaders.

Vision

To be a premier institution recognized for its academic rigor, innovative teaching, and commitment to student success.

Values

  • Academic Excellence
  • Personal Growth
  • Community Engagement
  • Diversity and Inclusion
  • Sustainability

History

GNYANODAYA EDUCATION TRUST was founded in 1998 by SHRI RAMKRISHNA KAUDIRAM VERMA & SMT. CHAMPA RAMKRISHNA VERMA . Since then, the college has grown to become a institution with a strong reputation for academic excellence. We are proud to have a diverse student body representing all 50 states and over 100 countries.
