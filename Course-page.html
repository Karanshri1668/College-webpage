Prerequisites

  • Mathematics
    Strong mathematics skills, including algebra, geometry, and trigonometry, are essential for success in BSc IT programs. These skills are needed for understanding programming concepts, algorithms, and data structures.

  • Science
    A background in science, particularly physics, is also beneficial for BSc IT students. Science courses provide a foundation for understanding the physical principles underlying computer systems and networks.

  • English
    Strong English language skills are important for effective communication and writing in the field of IT. Students should be able to clearly articulate technical concepts and write well-structured documents, such as reports and code documentation.
